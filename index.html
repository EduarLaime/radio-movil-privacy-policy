Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Radio Movil Express La Bella ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Permissions Used

Our app requires the following Android permissions to function properly:

  • INTERNET: Required to communicate with our servers and provide real-time services.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to detect internet connectivity and improve app reliability.
  • CAMERA: Used only when the user chooses to take a photo for their profile or verification purposes.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: Allows selecting images from the device when uploading a profile picture.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used only for saving temporary images if necessary (Android may ignore this permission in newer versions).
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Required to detect the user's location for taxi dispatch, navigation, and service accuracy.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: Used to allow the app to update the driver's location even when the app is minimized (only for driver mode, if applicable).

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your usage, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, profile photo
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location (with your explicit consent)
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage Data: Crash logs, diagnostic information
  • Media: Images that you voluntarily upload (profile or verification)

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used to:

  • Provide taxi dispatch and location-based services
  • Allow drivers and passengers to identify each other
  • Improve safety and prevent fraud
  • Enhance app performance and reliability
  • Comply with legal or operational requirements

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. Data may be shared securely with trusted service providers such as:

  • Firebase (authentication, storage, notifications)
  • Google Cloud (backend services)
  • Analytics providers

5. User Rights

You may request the modification or deletion of your data by contacting us at eduarlaime@outlook.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
